What is a project?

If you see a post with the tag "Project" is because is a job with a defined time and scope, a freelance one.

What if I do not find a job that fits my profile?

Share your profile with the community. Ask (Darshana's team will also help) to recruiters in the community and fellow talent to find more job posts. You can also look for help to enhance your profile.

What if I do not find a talent that fits the job description?

Share what you are looking for with the community. Ask (Darshana's team will also help) to talent and fellow recruiters in the community to onboard more talent. You can also keep participating and helping talent that you can hire after.
