How do I earn tokens?

You are a member of the community, created your profile on our website with a wallet. Your participation on Discord will be rewarded if you post information that helps the community or help other directly. We will add specific bounties in the upcoming weeks. The rewards are reviewed once a week.

How do I use tokens?

Tokens are cryptocurrency tokens from Algorand (USDC or Algos), so you can keep them on your wallet to later use then for premium services on our platform, change it for other tokens in an Exchange or cash them out to your currency.
